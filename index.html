BlueStone Jewellery Store - A Stellar Omnichannel Presence

BlueStone, founded in 2011 by Gaurav Singh Kushwaha in Bangalore, is one of India's largest e-commerce portal for fine jewellery. By seamlessly integrating online and physical retail channels, BlueStone has transformed the way consumers experience jewellery shopping.

Redefining the Jewellery Shopping Experience

At BlueStone, we're dedicated to enhancing your jewellery shopping experience with unmatched convenience through a highly developed team that ensures every question about your products gets answered. We also have a Lifetime Exchange and Buyback Policy.

7000+ Certified Jewellery Designs

Our jewellery is certified by prestigious authorities such as BIS Hallmark, SGL, IGI, and GSI to ensure the authenticity and quality of every piece. Our extensive range of 7000+ contemporary creations tells a unique story, each inspired by different facets of life.
